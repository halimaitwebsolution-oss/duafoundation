Who We Are

Dua Foundation

Dua Foundation is a non-political voluntary welfare organization dedicated to achieving the highest ideals, goals, and objectives for the betterment of our community. Founded on the principles of compassion, service, and unity, we strive to uplift those in need and create lasting positive change.

The foundation shall awaken mutual cooperation, sympathy, and unity among all its members. Together, we work towards building a society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Through our diverse programs spanning education, healthcare, community development, and cultural engagement, we are committed to improving the living standards of the helpless and the poor while fostering a spirit of togetherness and shared responsibility.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

The guiding principles that drive every initiative and shape our commitment to community welfare and national development.

Our Mission

To run as a non-political voluntary welfare organization, working tirelessly towards improving the living standards of the helpless and the poor. We aim to provide essential support, resources, and opportunities to those who need them the most.

Our Vision

The overall development of the area and welfare of the society. We envision a community where quality education reaches every corner, scholarships empower poor and meritorious students, and every family lives with dignity and hope.

Our Values

Dedication, honesty, impartiality, and sincerity for the welfare of our country and nation. These core values guide every decision we make and every action we take in service of our community.

What We Stand For

Core Principles

The foundational pillars that define our identity and guide our path towards meaningful community development.

1

Non-Political Organization

The organization shall avoid all forms of political involvement and remain strictly non-partisan. However, members will actively participate during national emergencies to serve the greater good of the country.

2

Community Unity

Fostering mutual cooperation, sympathy, and understanding among all members. We believe that unity is the foundation of meaningful progress and lasting social change.

3

Social Welfare

Dedicated to improving the living standards of the helpless and the poor through targeted programs, direct assistance, and sustainable community development initiatives.

4

Cultural Engagement

Organizing gatherings, Eid reunions, Milad Mahfil, and cultural programs that strengthen communal bonds and celebrate our shared heritage and traditions.

5

Preventing Child Marriage

Taking an active role in preventing child marriage within the community through awareness campaigns, education, and direct intervention to protect the rights and futures of children.

6

Anti-Dowry Commitment

All members are firmly committed not to give or take dowry. We stand against this harmful practice and actively work to eliminate it from our society.

7

National Service

Playing an active role during national emergencies by mobilizing resources, volunteers, and community support to serve the country when it needs us the most.

8

Adaptive Growth

Embracing change and adopting new voluntary programs with the approval of the general assembly, ensuring that the organization evolves to meet the ever-changing needs of the community.
