What We Do

Building a Better Community

Dua Foundation is committed to creating lasting positive change through a wide range of welfare activities. From community development and education to healthcare and agricultural programs, our initiatives are designed to uplift the underprivileged and foster a spirit of unity, cooperation, and shared prosperity. Explore our current endeavors and our vision for the future below.

Voluntary Welfare

A non-political voluntary welfare organization dedicated to achieving noble ideals and meaningful goals for the betterment of society.

Current

Community Unity

Awakening mutual cooperation, sympathy, and understanding among members to strengthen the bonds of our community.

Current

Cultural Programs

Organizing forest feasts, Eid reunions, Milad Mahfil, Seeratunnabi celebrations, sports events, and drama performances to enrich cultural life.

Current

Social Welfare

Dedicated programs to improve the living standards of helpless and poor people through direct assistance and sustainable support.

Current

Non-Political Stand

Maintaining a strictly non-political stance while remaining active and responsive during national emergencies when duty calls.

Current

Preventing Child Marriage

Taking an active role in preventing child marriage through awareness campaigns, community education, and direct intervention.

Current

Anti-Dowry

All members are firmly committed to neither giving nor taking dowry, setting a powerful example for social change.

Current

Adaptive Programs

Adopting new voluntary welfare programs as needed, with the approval of the general assembly, ensuring responsive community service.

Current

Education & Scholarships

Establishing a special fund to provide scholarships to poor and meritorious students, empowering the next generation through education.

Future

National & International Days

Observing important national and international days with due dignity and respect to foster patriotism and global awareness.

Future

Orphan Welfare

Comprehensive programs for orphans, helpless and disabled individuals, alongside anti-drug awareness campaigns for a healthier society.

Future

Charitable Hospital

Establishing a charitable hospital with government permission to provide affordable healthcare services to the underserved community.

Future

Agricultural Development

Promoting fish farming, organic farming, tree plantation, cattle and goat farming, and poultry to build sustainable livelihoods.

Future

Social Service

Vaccination drives, anti-dowry campaigns, adult education programs, and self-employment initiatives for community empowerment.

Future

Social Forestry

Social forestry programs alongside efforts to establish electricity, gas, and telephone connections for rural development.

Featured Program

Agricultural Development Initiative

Our agricultural development initiative aims to transform rural livelihoods through sustainable farming practices and diversified income sources. By equipping community members with modern techniques and resources, we envision a self-sufficient, prosperous future.

This program is designed to create long-term economic stability while preserving our natural environment for generations to come.

  • Fish farming and aquaculture training
  • Organic farming and sustainable agriculture
  • Tree plantation and reforestation drives
  • Cattle, goat, and poultry farming support
  • Market access and fair trade partnerships
